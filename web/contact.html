❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How much can I earn?

Earnings vary based on ad type and availability. Users typically earn $1-$15 per day.

How do I withdraw?

After watching 50 ads, you can withdraw USDC to any Base chain wallet. Minimum withdrawal is $1. There are zero fees — the platform pays all gas costs.

What wallets are supported?

Any wallet that supports Base chain (Coinbase Wallet, MetaMask, Trust Wallet, etc.).

Is my data safe?

Yes. We only collect your Google account name and email for authentication. We never sell your data.

How long do withdrawals take?

Instant! Withdrawals are processed via smart contracts on the Base blockchain.
