About AttentionPay

Our Mission

AttentionPay is on a mission to fairly compensate users for their attention. In today's digital world, your attention is one of the most valuable resources — and we believe you should be paid for it.

How It Works

AttentionPay connects advertisers with users who are willing to watch ads, complete surveys, and try new apps. In return, users earn real USDC (a stablecoin pegged to the US dollar) on the Base blockchain.

Why USDC on Base?

  • Zero fees: Base chain has extremely low transaction costs
  • Instant payouts: Withdrawals are processed via smart contracts
  • Transparent: All transactions are verifiable on the blockchain
  • Global: Anyone, anywhere can receive USDC

Our Values

  • Fairness: Users deserve a share of the revenue their attention generates
  • Transparency: Clear earning rates, no hidden fees
  • Privacy: Minimal data collection, no phone numbers required
  • Accessibility: Available worldwide, works on any device

For Advertisers

AttentionPay offers a unique opportunity to reach engaged users who are actively watching and interacting with ads. Our platform ensures genuine views and measurable results.
